About Us

Think Global,
Act Local

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged children and communities across India. We believe that every child deserves access to education, opportunity, and a brighter tomorrow.

Founded by a group of passionate young individuals, we work at the grassroots level — reaching the students who need us most. From free tutoring sessions to skill workshops and crisis support, NayePankh means "New Wings" — and that's exactly what we give.

What We Do

Our Programs

Four pillars that hold up our mission of change.

📖

Education Support

Free tutoring, study materials, and academic mentoring for children from low-income families. We make sure no child falls behind due to a lack of resources.

Learn more →
🛠️

Skill Development

Workshops on digital literacy, communication, leadership, and vocational skills to prepare youth for the real world and future employment.

Learn more →
🌐

Community Outreach

Health camps, awareness drives, and community events that bring people together and address local challenges at the ground level.

Learn more →
🆘

Crisis Support

Emergency relief, mental health resources, and rapid response assistance for families and children during times of distress or natural disasters.
